Pokémon GO Shared AR Experience

A Feature in Pokémon GO Enabling Realtime AR Multiplayer Experience

About

In Pokémon GO, each player can set one Pokémon in their inventory as their buddy, with which they can play in vairous ways in both the game and AR. The shared AR experience in buddy feature allows up to three users' buddy to coexist in one single AR session in real time and play with them including petting and feeding. On top of that, users can use group photo mode to have all pokemon buddy in the session look at their cameras and play animations so that users can take nice group photos. This feature has been shipped in Dec 2019.
