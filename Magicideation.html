Magic Ideation

A Mixed Reality (MR) APP for Spatial Brainstorming

About

Magic ideation is a project our team initiated in the AT&T Magic Leap Mixed Reality Hackathon over a weekend. It's a productivity tool enabling users to ideate in mixed reality in an expressive and efficient way. It allows users to create, visualize and interact with notes of different media including text, image, audio, video, 3D map, 3D models, and freehand drawing. Users can also create a semantic hierarchy and mindmap of notes. Further down the line, it paves the way for a remote collaborative ideation experience.
With this project, we won the 3rd place in the hackathon out of 300+ teams. Currently, this project is at an early stage and still under further development.

Brainstorming and Its Pain Points

Brainstorming, or ideation is usually the first and foremost step of our thinking process applicable to all daily work, study, and life. Traditional means of ideation usually includes real-world whiteboarding and digital ideation using 2D media like laptop and smartphone.

Whiteboarding
The real-world whiteboarding goes with the advantage of allowing natural and expressive freehand drawing, which is a convenient and intimate experience. You can write or draw anything you want on a whiteboard as you wish. However,

  • It's constrained to the requirement of attendees' physical presence or co-presence.
  • Also, complex ideation could easily require the involvement of rich media like image, audio, video, 3D model and so on, which couldn't be supported by traditional whiteboarding.
  • Other than that, saving, sharing and re-editing are particularly hard for whiteboard ideation.

2D Digital Device Based Ideation
By introducing 2D digital devices like a laptop or smartphone in the ideation process, a user can easily incorporate media of different types as carriers of ideas. Aside from that, it's easy to share, edit and save ideation on your phone or computer. However, a 2D screen-based digital tools suffer from their inherited disadvantage

  • A limited canvas
  • The lack of expressiveness and convenience

Solution

Our mixed reality ideation tool combines the merits of the two brainstorming means above and brings it to a new level.

  • Firstly, mixed reality adds an extra dimension to the experience providing users with an infinite spatial canvas to create and display information in the space in a more expressive way.
  • Secondly, users can make connections between notes and organize ideas in different media formats to create a map of ideas through a set of intuitive and efficient spatial interactions utilizing different input sources, which introduces a more expressive way of information interaction.
  • Further down the line, it paves the way for a revolutionary remote collaborative ideation.
