Wanderful

An Augmented Reality and Location Based Mobile Social Application

About

"Wanderful" is a side project we initiated in 2017. We are a group of people passionate about virtual and augmented reality and their potential to revolutionize the way we consume information. The release of ARkit and ARcore have empowered mobile phones in our hands to interact with the world in a way never possible before. Based on which, we created "Wanderful", an AR and location-based mobile application to facilitate your interactions with friends and make new friends in a new way.

What's Next?

There have been so many online social applications coming to the market, which are getting more and more similar to each other with the same general functions and paradigm. No significant change has occurred for a long time.

However, with the evolution of technologies(AI, IOT, XR), we can’t help asking: “How can we further enhance social interactions and bring it to a new level? What’s next?”

User Research

Target Users

A comprehensive comparative analysis was conducted over 30 popular social mobile apps in China and the United States. Meanwhile, to understand users' needs and the limit of current social applications, we interviewed over 40 young people in China and America of different professions to understand their daily online/offline socialization needs and activities hence to recognize their socialization patterns.

The Marriage of Mobile AR ans Social

Augmented reality(AR) makes the real world around users interactive and digitally manipulable, which has great potential for enhancing how we interact with our surroundings as well as people in it. With the release of two mobile AR framework(ARkit and ARcore), a new dimension of mobile phone interaction has become accessible to millions of smartphone users. The surrounding understanding and motion tracking capabilities coming with the AR frameworks could open a new door for the next generation of mobile social applications corresponding to our research findings from three aspects:

  • Mobile AR enables people to interact with the real world in many fun ways, which could potentially facilitate more offline social interactions
  • Location-based socialization could evolve into surrounding-based, which makes socializations more efficient and intriguing
  • With its utility nature, AR can connect socializations to other aspects of life in the future

Feature One - 360° Image Portal

Sharing images and videos on social media is the most popular behavior on almost every social application. 360° images are getting more and more popular with its advantage of capturing and sharing moments in a more immersive way. Our app doesn't only allow users to upload and share 360° images but also enables users to view them like no way before. By utilizing the motion tracking function, users can hold their phones and wander inside 360° images as if they actually enter the space of the image through a portal. Thus, we call this feature "The 360° Image Portal".

Live Demo of 360° Image Portal

Feature Two - Real-World Emoji Message

Common ground and experience usually bring people closer to each other: people who have been to the same place potentially could have more common interests and daily intersections. With the Real-World Emoji Message function, you can leave emoji messages in anywhere you go including restaurants, theaters, parks and so forth. For other people who come later, they will be able to see the emoji message left by you through the camera of their phone and interact with those messages.
