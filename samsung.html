Samsung Health Motivation Center

A concept design for Samsung Health

About

Samsung Health is the service provided on Samsung smartphones to help users track their fitness data. Samsung Health Motivation Center is a side project based on Samsung Health application in collaboration with another teammate. As Samsung Health users, they could experience lack of motivation to keep our workout habit.
With that in mind, we design this Samsung Health Motivation Center to integrate into Samsung Health. By utilizing emerging technologies (Virtual and Augmented Reality), we believe this motivation center can create a more innovative and engaging experience to motivate users' workout activities.

This project includes design and prototyping across different platforms from 2D to 3D including: mobile, VR, AR and wearable devices.

Findings

In the user research, our study subjects include people at different workout stage. Thus, based on our research results, we divided users into four different workout stages as follow, which together compose the complete user journey. For subjects in different stage, different obstacles were revealed.

Among all the study subjects, most of them fall into the stage of "Start Workout". Thus, we have decided to target users at such stage.
For users starting workout, boredom and unoticeable improvement were proved to be the major pain points, which become the problem we decided to focus on.

Comparative Analysis

To understand the focus of our product, a comprehensive comparative analysis was conducted. We evaluated 9 comparative products over 10 dimensions. What we have concluded is that leveraging emerging technologies to design a user-friendly and stimulative service will be a new breakthrough in this market.

Solution

As Illustrated by our research, lack of motivation is the problem troubling most users and mattering most. To motivate users to stick to their fitness plan, we designed the Samsung Motivation Center into Samsung Health service. The Samsung Health Motivation Center is an open platform integrating innovative, stimulative ways and emerging technologies(Virtual/Augmented Reality) to make the workout process more engaging.

The Samsung Health Motivation Center is an open and growing platform integrating innovative applications to motivate and user's workout activities and improve their workout experience by providing auxiliary service. In the homepage, all the applications will be listed and sorted. Currently, there are three built-in applications designed: VR Cycling, Running City and Progress Gallery.

VR Cycling

According to our user research, riding the stationary bike is one of the most popular way of workout among people across different age groups, which can effectively improve our cardio-pulmonary function. However, such an exercise could be tedious and hard to persist. By combining Virtual Reality with Stationary biking, users will be more engaged in the workout process, thus the goal of motivating users can be achieved.

After selecting the virtual scene to ride in, the user can just insert their phone into Gear VR and begin their cycling in the virtual world.

Cross-Platform Social Cycling

Group exercise is always more motivating for most people than an isolated experience. In VR cycling, you are not doing it on your own. You can invite your friends or join stranger groups to cycle together in the virtual world. Furthermore, to accommodate users on different platforms, the application allows users on both VR platform and other 2D platforms, like mobile phone and tablet, to enjoy cycling together.
